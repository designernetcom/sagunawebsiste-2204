about us

Trusted people in Plastic Raw Material Suppliers

Founded in 1974, SAGUNA GROUP has established itself as a beacon of trust and excellence in the trading of plastic raw materials, polymer products, and allied goods for the irrigation and water pump sectors. With over 50 years of unwavering commitment to quality and innovation, we have become a name synonymous with reliability and leadership. Our legacy, spanning three generations, is deeply rooted in strong business ethics, transparent dealings, and a relentless pursuit of customer satisfaction. From humble beginnings to a commanding presence in the global market, our journey is a testament to resilience, growth, and an enduring vision for the future.
Today, SAGUNA GROUP stands as a leader in delivering sustainable solutions that empower industries and foster long-term success. With an expansive portfolio, a value-driven approach, and a steadfast dedication to timely service, we are shaping the future of the polymer industry while honoring the legacy of our past. As we celebrate five decades of excellence, we remain committed to driving innovation, forging meaningful partnerships, and building a sustainable tomorrow for businesses across the globe.
