SAGUNA GROUP - A Legacy of Trust and Excellence Since 1974

With a legacy spanning over three generations, SAGUNA GROUP has emerged as a leading force in the fields of polymer, irrigation, and water pump industries. Based in Pune, this powerhouse of trading excellence has earned its reputation as a strong and trusted name, offering an extensive range of plastic raw materials and allied products to various plastic manufacturing industries across India.
Our strength lies in our deep-rooted business knowledge, inherited ethics, and an unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. For over four decades, we have stood at the forefront of innovation and reliability, ensuring that our clients receive only the best in the industry.
